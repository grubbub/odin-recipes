Casserole

It's mostly just melting a bunch of stuff in a dish and calling it a casserole. See Tuna Casserole if you don't believe me. Seriously. Cooking like a Grandma y'all! Whatever's in the pantry!

Ingredients

  • Meats
  • Starch
    • Like lasagna noodles, or elbow noodles
    • Seriously, it's just stuff in a pan
  • Sauce
    • Can seriously be absolutley anything, somebody probably used ranch in one.
      • I'm done guys.
  • You can put some vegetables in there, no one cares at this point

Steps

  1. Put the things you have collected in an indestructible pan
  2. Melt everything
    • You'll probably burn the top, don't worry about it, call it a "crust".
  3. Serve it to all you love or hate, doesn't matter
